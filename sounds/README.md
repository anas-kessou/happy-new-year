# Sound Files Required

Place your audio files in this folder with the following names:

1. **countdown-tick.mp3** - A tick/beep sound for the last 10 seconds of countdown

   - Suggested: Short tick or beep sound (< 1 second)

2. **heartbeat.mp3** - Heartbeat sound for the heart animation

   - Suggested: Realistic heartbeat sound (lub-dub), can be looped

3. **firework.mp3** - Firework explosion sound

   - Suggested: Short firework burst/explosion sound (< 2 seconds)

4. **romantic-song.mp3** - Romantic background music for the gallery
   - Suggested: A soft romantic instrumental or your favorite love song

## Where to get free sounds:

- **Pixabay** (https://pixabay.com/sound-effects/) - Free sound effects
- **Freesound** (https://freesound.org/) - Free sounds with various licenses
- **Mixkit** (https://mixkit.co/free-sound-effects/) - Free sound effects

## Recommended sounds:

- Countdown: Search for "countdown beep" or "timer tick"
- Heartbeat: Search for "heartbeat" or "heart beating"
- Firework: Search for "firework explosion" or "firework burst"
- Romantic: Search for "romantic piano" or "love song instrumental"
